Method: COGS is built bottom-up — six per-unit cost categories (Item Cost, Packaging, Handling Fees, Inbound Shipping/Freight, Outbound Shipping/Freight, Other Related Costs) × units sold per customer type — plus an allocated share of shared/overhead COGS from the ledger. When the NPD link is on, Item, Packaging and Inbound costs are sourced live from the NPD Costs tab (item cost from Formula/Fill orders, packaging from Jar/Box/Label orders, shipping split per unit across all NPD units). Effective COGS % is derived, so margins reflect real landed unit economics. Operating Profit = Gross Profit − Direct OPEX − Allocated OPEX, allocated pro-rata by revenue. CAPEX (£0.00) sits outside operating profit. All figures monthly-equivalent.
